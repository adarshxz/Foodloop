## âš™ï¸ How It Works

1. **List**: A donor posts details about surplus food (quantity, type, best before).
2. **Notify**: FoodLoop sends notifications to nearby users.
3. **Reserve**: A recipient views the listing and reserves the food.
4. **Pickup**: The recipient follows the map to collect the food.
5. **Impact**: Both parties rate the experience, and sustainability metrics are updated.

How FoodLoop Works

Five simple steps from waste to worth

1

Donor Lists Surplus

Event organizers, hostels, or canteens list available surplus food with photos, quantity, and pickup details.

2

Platform Notifies Users

FoodLoop instantly sends push notifications to nearby students, NGOs, and community members.

3

Recipient Claims Food

Users browse available listings, reserve meals, and receive pickup location and timing.

4

Pickup is Tracked

Real-time tracking ensures smooth coordination between donors and recipients.

5

Impact is Measured

Both parties rate the experience, and sustainability metrics update automatically.
